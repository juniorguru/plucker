Úvodní představení

esc Aerospace se zabývá elektronikou se zvláštním důrazem na embedded systémy, přenos dat a mikrovlnné aplikace. Vyvíjí několik produkčních řad bezpilotních letounů UAV i antidronové systémy C-UAS. Výzkumné aktivity společnosti se zaměřují na oblasti bezposádkových řídících systémů, antikolizních systémů a systémů pro pozemní stanice řízení bezpilotních letounů a satelitů. esc Aerospace se aktivně účastní vesmírných projektů, včetně projektů vypisovaných Evropskou vesmírnou agenturou ESA a evropskou obrannou agenturou EDA. V této oblasti probíhají práce na letovém software pro satelitní palubní systémy, EGSE software a zpracování dat.

Pracovní nabídka

Who We Are

At our ESC Aerospace Company, we focus on product development in electronics, with particular emphasis on embedded systems, and data transmission.

The company’s research activities focus on unmanned control systems, anti-collision systems, and systems for ground control stations for drones and satellites including development in several production lines of UVA drones and C-UAS anti-drone systems.

Our company is actively involved in space projects, including projects announced by the European Space Agency and the European Defence Agency. In the area of Aerospace, we are working on flight software for satellite on-board systems, qualified space electronics, EGSE software, and data processing.

Areas of Our Projects

  • Satellite Systems

- Development of flight software for satellite systems
- Qualified space electronics
- Testing of critical software and communication interfaces

  • Avionics UAS/RPAS

- Development and testing of UAS/RPAS unmanned aircraft, drone revitalization
- Preparation and integration of systems for use in non-segregated airspace
- Testing of onboard software and communication interfaces
- Participation in building the ground segment for receiving data from UAS/RPAS and satellites

  • Medical

- Technology development in the field of biomedicine
- Product development in the field of medical robotics
- Opportunity to lead projects focusing on medical software and hardware

What You’ll Bring to Us

  • Secondary school diploma or university degree in technical fields
  • Software Engineering skills especially embedded C programming and debugging
  • Solid experience with system design and integration, particularly in the areas of the realization of testing and troubleshooting
  • Understanding of RTOS operating systems, CAN/UART/SPI/I2C interfaces
  • Strong analytical and proactive approach to solving problems
  • Good teamwork and collaborative skills
  • Good spoken and written technical English
  • Experience in the following areas is an advantage:
  • HW design
  • Python, C++
  • CANDF, Ethernet
  • Agile software development/V-Model knowledge
  • MISRA Standard
  • Work with oscilloscope/logic analyzer
  • STM microcontrollers

What We Offer

  • International and well-established environment
  • Chance to work with European brands in the aerospace area, including European Space Agency, Airbus, LUX Space, and Thales
  • Offices in Prague and Brno
  • Agile and open-minded culture, with high levels of trust and flexibility
  • Opportunity for professional growth and development
  • Possibility to learn new and cutting-edge technologies in an environment that encourages new ideas
  • Assistance with possible relocation
  • Stable and long-term cooperation

Immediate start desireable

In case you are interested, please send us your CV along with your picture and financial expectations.
