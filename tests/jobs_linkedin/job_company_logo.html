Kateřina Růžičková

Finance & Operations manager ve…

Roivenue is a data marketing platform and our mission is to translate data into knowledge for smarter decisions. Currently we are helping companies in nine countries on two continents to optimize budgets across dozens of markets. And we want more. 


After a fair share of start-up adventures, ROIVENUE is now going through a major reboot phase and is getting ready for scaling up. We want to approach as many e-commerce companies as possible with a new product - Data Connectors. That's where you come in.


WHAT WILL YOU DO?

  • Maintenance of our data source connectors (a.g. Google Analytics, Facebook, AdWords, +many more)
  • Development of new data connectors
  • Optimisation and improvement of existing procedures
  • Identification of issues connected to data processing
  • Fixing maintenance issues and bugs
  • Participation on new feature development
  • Maintenance of project Azure infrastructure (cost management, performance, security)


ABOUT YOU

We are looking for a junior - knowledge is important but work experience is not necessary. Any private or school project is a good reference for us.


Must haves

  • .NET platform experience
  • C# knowledge
  • data oriented mind
  • CZ native speaker preferred, EN is a must


Nice to haves

  • Experience with HTTP protocol
  • Experiencee with Microsoft Azure
  • online marketing awareness
  • located in CR, Prague


THE OFFER

  • A great product and competitive salary
  • A one off signing bonus of 5 000 CZK that can be used to furnish and supply your home office.
  • An opportunity to earn employee of the month bonuses.
  • Participation in our company stock option plan for every full-time member of the team.
  • Access to a VIP Datart account
  • An endless source of know how, productivity hints, and an experienced mentor in our Product team. Company books to use and study.
  • A great group of people with team spirit in a start-up work environment and endless growth opportunities
  • The opportunity to be on the ground floor in an exciting, expanding data industry.
  • An unlimited number of free COVID tests, Company beer/BBQ every month, a fridge full of beers after 5 PM, plus occasional board game evenings and other activities - see our Instagram for more!


Sounds great, right? Send your CV and a motivation letter and let's get started! 


Not enough experience? Check our internship offer (suitable both for students and professionals new to our industry) that’s designed to groom future Roivenue stars.
